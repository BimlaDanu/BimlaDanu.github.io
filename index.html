Hallo!

    My research focus is on the theoretical and computational study of strongly correlated electron systems. In general, using tools from quantum many-body physics and statistical mechanics, we seek to understand and characterize different phases and properties of correlated quantum matter. Examples include the magnetism, Kondo effect, heavy fermions, spin liquids, quantum criticality, quantum Hall effect, high-temperature superconductivity, and related physical phenomena. .
