Hello and Welcome ! My research focuses on the theoretical and computational study of strongly correlated electron systems. Using tools from quantum many-body physics and statistical mechanics, I aim to understand and characterize different phases and properties of correlated quantum matter. Examples include magnetism, Kondo effect, heavy fermions, spin liquids, quantum criticality, quantum Hall effect, and high-temperature superconductivity.

Areas of Research & Interest

  • Theoretical quantum condensed matter physics
  • Strongly correlated electron systems
  • Computational many-body physics: I have particularly hands-on experience in the following areas
    • Auxiliary field quantum Monte Carlo
    • Tensor network methods
    • Lanczos exact diagonalizations
